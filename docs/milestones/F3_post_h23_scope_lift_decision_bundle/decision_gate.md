true:

- the true executor boundary is localized on the current endpoint, with
  `R32_d0_family_local_boundary_sharp_zoom` completed or explicitly replaced by
  a later same-endpoint packet that materially updates the frozen `H23`
  boundary story;

  deferred `R33_d0_non_retrieval_overhead_localization_audit` route, with
  `R29_d0_same_endpoint_systems_recovery_execution_gate` still blocked unless a
  later explicit packet reauthorizes it;

- a later refreeze explicitly reauthorizes scope lift.

- `R32_d0_family_local_boundary_sharp_zoom` remains the primary justified next
  science lane;
- `R33_d0_non_retrieval_overhead_localization_audit` remains the deferred
  systems-audit prerequisite lane;
- `R29_d0_same_endpoint_systems_recovery_execution_gate` remains blocked and
  cannot be used as a shortcut to scope lift;
- `F3` remains planning-only and cannot authorize widened execution or broader
  narrative by itself.

`R31_d0_same_endpoint_systems_recovery_reauthorization_packet`, nor
