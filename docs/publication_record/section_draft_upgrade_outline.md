# Section Draft Upgrade Outline

Status: record of the structural manuscript pass executed after the bundle-
first assembly phase.

## Decision

The post-bundle manuscript pass focused on turning the current bundle into a
more paper-shaped section draft, not on line-edit polish alone. That pass has
now been executed on `manuscript_bundle_draft.md`, and this note remains as the
structural rationale for later polish-only passes.

## Why this comes before polish

The current manuscript bundle already contains most of the paper-grade claim
content, but several sections still mix prose with planning scaffolds such as
`Main items`, `Boundary note`, or layout reminders. Polishing sentences before
that structure is converted into section-shaped draft text would create churn
without reducing the main remaining gap. The higher-value step is to promote
the current bundle from a strong structured draft into a section draft that
looks more like a paper manuscript and less like an internal assembly ledger.

## Upgrade targets

### 1. Introduction and claim ladder

- absorb `Main items` and `Boundary note` into section prose;
- decide whether the claim-ladder figure/table pair should be referenced at the
  end of the opening section or split across introduction and threats.

### 2. Methods

- split methods into two paper-shaped subsections:
  - trace substrate / semantics
  - exact retrieval / geometry signal
- keep the asymptotic geometry note descriptive rather than promotional.

### 3. Executor results

- split current results into:
  - executor branches and negative controls
  - mask dependence and failure provenance
- convert the current figure references into sentence-level figure callouts.

### 4. Precision and systems

- keep `R1` as a compact bounded-positive section with one explicit boundary
  sentence;
- keep `R2` as a gate section with quantitative prose and no standalone main-
  text table unless later layout needs one.

### 5. Compiled boundary and threats

- turn the `D0` section into a clean endpoint narrative with explicit appendix
  references for memory-surface and stress/reference companions;
- keep threats as a short, explicit boundary section rather than a catch-all
  discussion dump.

### 6. Appendix

- keep the appendix prose short and functional;
- move regeneration commands and artifact grouping into compact subsections
  instead of long narrative blocks.

## Completion check

- main-text sections now read as paper prose first and planning scaffold
  second;
- section-level figure/table references now appear in prose rather than only in
  bullets;
- no section upgrade introduces broader claims than the current frozen ledgers
  support;
- the release summary can continue to derive from the manuscript bundle without
  forcing another public-surface wording rewrite.
