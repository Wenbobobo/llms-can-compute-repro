- merge-main discussion stays planning-only;
- advisory GPTPro/Origin material remains advisory-only;

  `compiled_online_exact_retrieval_primitive_or_attention_coprocessor_route`.

- the parked root checkout is explicitly quarantined;
- clean-descendant promotion prep is explicit and separate from merge-main;
- Origin and GPTPro advisory text is available in the clean line;
